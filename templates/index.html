What is this?

The Aramaic Root Atlas lets you search for any Semitic triliteral root and see every word form derived from it across multiple Aramaic corpora — from the Peshitta New Testament and Old Testament to Biblical Aramaic and beyond.

Each root card shows Hebrew and Arabic cognates, semantic bridges between related roots, and bilingual glosses in English and Spanish.
